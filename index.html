Take Action

Now is the time to take action, to flee. Sometimes you need to literally run and take action over the temptation and desires of sin. Here are a few suggestions.

Go for a walk

Get outside and be active. While you are on a walk, listen or watch this spoken word entitled "Sincerly Freedom"

After listening, take time to turn towards God in prayer.

Declare Truth

Go back through the content of this site and recite scripture outloud. There is power in the spoken word of God. Remember, this is literal word of God, His words breathed out. Soak that in and bodly declare His truths over your life. Revel in the glory of the cross. You are free from sin, Christ has already paid the price, rejoice in that freedom!

Seek Repentance

Repent of your sins and turn to Jesus. Confess to Him your sinful desires, He is there listening and waiting patiently for you to turn to him.

Find Accountability

Seek out other Christians. Confess your struggles to someone you trust and ask them to keep you accountable and assure that they will always point you to Jesus.

Praise God. He has already sent his Son to take the punishment that we deserve. Let the truth of the gospel sink in deeply. Remember who Christ is and what he has already done for you. You are free in Christ, rejoice in the freedom that Christ's death brings. Be encouraged and encourage those who are in this fight of purity. Rest in Jesus and depend completely on him.
